Questions & Answers

How long does the scent last? +

Our fragrances are crafted as Eau de Parfum. This means they have a high concentration of premium perfume oils. You can expect them to last 6 to 8 hours on your skin, and even longer on your clothes!

Is delivery really free? +

Yes! We offer 100% free delivery exclusively within Baybay City. Just make sure to pin your location during the checkout process so our rider can find you easily.

How do I track my order? +

It is very simple. Just scroll up to the "Order Status" section on this website and type in the exact 11-digit phone number you used when you placed your order. It will show you live updates from our team!
